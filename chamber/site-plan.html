01

Site Name

Abuja Chamber of Commerce

02

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a resource hub for businesses in Abuja, Nigeria. The site provides information on local events, networking opportunities, a business directory, and encourages residents to support local businesses. It aims to promote local economic growth, support small and medium enterprises, and foster a stronger sense of community among Abuja's business owners.

03

Target Market

Business owners, entrepreneurs, and residents in Abuja and the surrounding FCT (Federal Capital Territory) area.

04

Site Goals

  • Improve member engagement by providing valuable resources and networking opportunities.
  • Attract new businesses and encourage economic development within Abuja.
  • Improve the visibility and reputation of the chamber as a trusted authority on local business matters.
05

User Personas

A

Amina, 32

Small Business Owner

Owns a small fashion and textiles shop in Wuse Market. She is looking for networking opportunities, marketing support, and ways to connect with other local entrepreneurs to grow her customer base.

E

Emeka, 44

Corporate Executive

A senior manager at a logistics company considering expansion into Abuja. He is interested in partnership opportunities, economic development data, and networking with established local business leaders.

D

David, 26

New Resident

Recently relocated to Abuja for a new job. He is looking to learn about local services, find a directory of trustworthy businesses, and discover community events to help him settle in.

06

Scenarios

  • A local business owner is interested in joining the chamber to network with other business owners. They visit the site to learn about membership benefits, fees, and how to apply.
  • A community member wants to find upcoming events or workshops hosted by the chamber. They visit the site to browse an events calendar and register for something that interests them.
  • A visitor considering relocating to Abuja wants to understand the local business environment. They explore the chamber's site to review the business directory, available services, and general quality-of-life information.
07

SEO Plan

  • Use relevant keywords (e.g. "Abuja business directory," "Abuja chamber of commerce," "FCT small business") throughout site descriptions and content.
  • Verify the site on Google Business Profile.
  • Get inbound backlinks from member businesses to improve search rankings.
  • Embed Google Analytics across all site pages to track visitor behavior.
